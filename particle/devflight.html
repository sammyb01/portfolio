Devflight

UX/UI Design (Desktop), Front-end Development

www.devflight.com

The experience design of this project was centrally focused on highlighting the companyâ€™s value proposition while maximizing the number of calls to action in order to streamline potential clients. In order to achieve this, the design includes buttons at the top and bottom of the body content center-aligned to guarantee user awareness. Along with the buttons, there are links in the header and footer as well, using different language to cast a wider net to potential clients. The layout is designed to completely cut any extraneous or distracting content.

The goal of the interface design was to be clean and simple, using color to pop the vital content. The black gradient background paired with the sans-serif font is subtle and contrasts well with the green accent used for specific text and buttons. The call to action buttons are highlighted using a green gradient and are designed to mirror Google Material Design buttons. The text is color-coded and specifically weighted to separate the statements into categories.
