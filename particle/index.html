Project 1

BMW Careers


My goal behind this project was to compress the original site in an intuitive and simple way. Once one of the buttons is clicked, the line in the middle slide animates to reveal the rest of the image as well as the text/interface for the selected category. In a more visually developed iteration, the technician half of the landing page would be an x-ray image that shows the engine and the insides of the car. This imagery would aid the viewer in understanding the functions of each button as well as making the very simple page visually interesting.
